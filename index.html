How to Play

  • W A S D or Arrow Keys — Fly around
  • Auto-attack — Your bat automatically bites the nearest enemy within range
  • E — Echo Blast: AoE ultimate that damages all nearby enemies (has a cooldown, shown in the teal bar)
  • U — Open the Upgrade menu to spend Skill Points on stats
  • H — Open this Help screen

Goal

Survive endless waves of hawks, spiders and hunters. Defeating enemies grants XP (levels you up and grants Skill Points) and gold. Spend Skill Points in the Upgrade menu on Vitality, Strength, Agility, Focus and Echo Power to grow stronger and push further.
