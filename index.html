How to Play

  • W A S D or Arrow Keys — Fly around
  • Auto-attack — Your bat automatically bites the nearest enemy within range (toggle it on/off with the T key or the Auto-Attack button)
  • Click an enemy — Manually attack any enemy within your attack range, even with auto-attack turned off
  • R — Fire an RPG (rocket-propelled grenade) at the nearest enemy for a big explosive hit (has a cooldown, shown in the orange bar)
  • E — Echo Blast: AoE ultimate that damages all nearby enemies (has a cooldown, shown in the teal bar)
  • U — Open the Upgrade menu to spend Skill Points on stats
  • H — Open this Help screen
  • P — Open the Coin Shop to buy skins and permanent upgrades with coins
  • B — Open the Bestiary to identify every creature you'll face
  • Esc — Pause the game (or close whichever menu is open)

Wormholes

A swirling 🌀 wormhole occasionally appears somewhere on the map. Fly into it to be whisked away to a different world with its own night-sky colors — and get a small HP and coin bonus for braving the trip!

Hiding

🌲 In the Midnight Forest, fly close to a tree and click it to hide. Enemies completely lose track of you while you're tucked away. Click the tree again (or fly too far from it) to come back out — a safe spot to catch your breath before diving back into the fight!

Goal

Survive endless waves of hawks, spiders and hunters. Defeating enemies grants XP (levels you up and grants Skill Points) and gold. Spend Skill Points in the Upgrade menu on Vitality, Strength, Agility, Focus and Echo Power to grow stronger and push further.

🛒 Coin Shop 0 coins

Earn coins by defeating enemies. Every skin and permanent upgrade costs 100 coins. Permanent upgrades apply forever, on every run. Special Styles are premium whole-game visual filters that cost more.
